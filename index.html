About the product

Designed for the
way you eat.

STACK is a precision-engineered food container made of three airtight cylindrical modules that click together into a single elegant form. Each layer is thermally insulated and individually sealed — so your snack stays crispy, your main stays warm, and your dessert stays sweet.
